### Key Risk: A/V Cost Management

This is the one area that can blow the budget. Mitigation strategies an experienced operator already knows:

1. **Negotiate the flat package** — Hotels will try per-item, per-day pricing. Push for an all-inclusive 3-day rate.
2. **Room block leverage** — "I'll guarantee 20 room nights if you include basic AV in the meeting space package."
3. **Bring your own for breakout rooms** — Portable projector ($400), JBL speaker ($200–$400), wireless mic ($100–$200). Hotel charges $500–$1,500/day for the same.
4. **Ask what's included** — Some hotels include basic PA and a screen in the room rental. Start from there.
5. **Outside AV vendor** — Some hotels allow it (with a surcharge). Local Orlando AV companies can undercut hotel pricing by 40–60%.
